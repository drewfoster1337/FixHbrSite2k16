Welcome to the website of FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) Team 1747, Harrison Boiler Robotics (HBR)

Our Message

We are a high school FIRST Robotics team comprised of students from Harrison High School in West Lafayette, Indiana. Besides building a robot in six weeks, our team is dedicated to spreading the wonders of science and technology throughout our community and we encourage students to become involved in the various FIRST programs. Don't think robotics is for you? Check out our website to discover the other areas our team is involved in!
